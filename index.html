Frequently asked questions

Good to know.

The questions we hear most often — and the answers.

Does my crew need their own account?
No. You create projects and share a read-only link per stage or view. Anyone with the link sees what's happening in realtime — no login, no app store, no hassle.
Is there a free trial?
No separate trial — you pay €12.95 from the first month. In return you get instant access to everything: unlimited projects, all views, shareable crew links. Not happy? One click in Stripe and you've cancelled, with no further charges.
Does it work on a backstage crew iPad?
Yes. Stagetimer runs in any modern browser — iOS Safari, Android Chrome, desktop, anything. The backstage and stage views are specifically designed to live permanently on a tablet or monitor backstage.
Can I manage multiple festivals at once?
Yes. Unlimited projects on every plan. A project can contain multiple stages and multiple days, so anything from a one-day conference to a three-day festival with 10+ stages runs on the same subscription.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
You keep access until the end of your last paid period. After that, we set the account to read-only for 30 days so you can export your data. We then delete the project data from our database.
Does it work without internet?
Stagetimer is realtime and syncs over the internet. The last state stays cached in the browser to bridge brief hiccups, but for live work we recommend a stable connection (4G or WiFi) on every device.
